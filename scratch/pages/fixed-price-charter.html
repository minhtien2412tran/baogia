FAQs About Popular Fixed-Price Private Jet Routes

What does "fixed price" mean for these routes?

Fixed price means each route has a set price by aircraft category. Choose the option that fits your trip, then submit your schedule and passenger details to proceed.

How do I choose the right aircraft category?

Use the route card to compare passenger capacity and comfort. Pick the category that matches your group size and preferences.

Can I choose a specific aircraft model?

You're booking an aircraft category. JETBAY sources a suitable aircraft that meets the category, route, and schedule.

Are prices shown one-way or round-trip?

Prices shown are one-way unless stated otherwise. You can select round-trip or multi-city during booking.

What if my route isn't listed?

Request a custom route and we'll propose options for your airports, timing, and preferences—24/7.
