JetVina – Premier Private Jet & Air Charter Services Across Asia Pacific

JetVina is a leading Vietnam-based private aviation company specializing in private jet charter, business aviation, and air charter solutions throughout Vietnam, Southeast Asia, Asia Pacific, the Middle East, Europe, and the Americas.

With operational expertise across major business and leisure destinations, JetVina delivers seamless access to a global network of aircraft while maintaining a strong focus on Vietnam, Singapore, and Southeast Asia. Whether you require an executive business flight, luxury leisure travel, group charter, cargo charter, or urgent aircraft solutions, our experienced team provides personalized support 24/7.
